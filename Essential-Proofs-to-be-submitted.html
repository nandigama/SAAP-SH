Sports Authority of Andhra Pradesh

This is Students' portal to submit Online Application for Sports Quota Admission



Scan your Documents

All of the proofs you are going to submit must be clearly scanned in digital format JPEG image or PNG image

The size of each scanned document must not exceed 1MB ( 1024 KB ). Re-size or Re-Save at appropriate resolution to acheive the required file size.

Please make sure your digital photograph follows the general guidelines.Photograph can not be older than 6 months from the date of submission. Front facing and no dark glasses. Face should be clearly visible. Photograph format should be in JPEG or PNG. Photograph should have a light and plain background.Upload size should be less than 300 KB.

Naming your Scanned Documents

Please Name each of your scanned documents with the following naming convention.

  • Photograph : FirstName_LastName_Photograph.png
  • 10th CLASS : FirstName_LastName_SSLC_Certificate.png
  • Intermediate : FirstName_LastName_Intermediate_Certificate.png
  • PAN Copy :FirstName_LastName_PAN_Card.png
  • Sports Certificate: FirstName_LastName_SportsEvent_Venue_Year__CertificateNumber.png
  • Etc.,
