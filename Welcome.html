This is Students' portal to submit Online Application for Sports Quota Admission



What is SAAP, Govt. of AP?

SAAP, Govt. of AP is the authoritative body that .. among other things, validates your sports quota eligibility.

This online process aims to streamline the sports quota application process and create transparency in the governance process in line with the government directive.
